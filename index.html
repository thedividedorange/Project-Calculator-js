Simple Calculator

Welcome to the Simple Calculator web application! This calculator allows you to perform basic arithmetic calculations right in your web browser. Whether you're at home, at work, or on the go, you can use this calculator anytime you need to crunch some numbers.

Entering Numbers: To enter numbers, simply click on the buttons labeled with digits (0-9). You can enter multiple digits to form larger numbers.

  1. Entering Numbers: To enter numbers, simply click on the buttons labeled with digits (0-9). You can enter multiple digits to form larger numbers.
  2. Performing Operations:Use the buttons labeled with mathematical symbols (+, -, ×, ÷) to perform addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division respectively. You can also perform other operations like percentage (%), square (x²), and reciprocal (1/x) using the designated buttons.
  3. Once you've entered numbers and selected the desired operation, simply click on the equals button (=) to see the result displayed on the screen.
  4. Clearing and Resetting:
    • If you need to clear the current entry, use the CE button.
    • To reset the calculator and start fresh, click on the C button.
    • If you make a mistake while entering a number, you can delete the last digit using the DEL button.
  5. Error Handling:Don't worry about making mistakes! The calculator handles division by zero and will display an error message if necessary.
